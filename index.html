PLANET SHIFT is a memory-game created for entertainment purposes.

Memory-games are known for some positive effects they can have sometimes on brain functions like attention, concentration or visual recognition.

The Game Rules

The player is allowed to flip maximum 2 cards which remain front-face up when they MATCH, otherwise they flip back front-face down.

After pairing all card sets from the game-board while the TIME-REMAINING is not up, the player gets VICTORY.

If the TIME-REMAINING is up before finishing pairing all card sets then it's GAME OVER.

Enjoy!
